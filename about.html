Early 2000s ‚Äì Conceptualization

Abaqulusi Community Radio was conceptualized in the early 2000s and formally registered in 2013 as a Non-Profit Organization under the Social Development Act of 1997. It was the brainchild of Mabhuki Nkosi, together with other founders, who introduced the idea to the community.

2016 ‚Äì Licensing

In 2016, the station secured both its broadcasting and spectrum licenses from ICASA.

2019‚Äì2021 ‚Äì Studios Completed

With funding from MDDA in 2019, the Production and On-Air Studios were completed in early 2021, enabling the station to broadcast permanently from August 2020.

2022 ‚Äì Self Broadcasting

In 2022, ACR received its CENCS Licence from ICASA, allowing it to self-broadcast instead of using Sentech.
